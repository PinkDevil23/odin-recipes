Vegan Mango Ice Cream

Description

The recipe can be found on All recipes Vegan Mango Icecream. The recipe is by Yoly.

This is what Yoly says about their recipe: "Quick and easy vegan mango ice cream that takes less than 5 minutes to make, and you don't need an ice cream maker. Plus, you can make a refreshing mango smoothie with this same recipe! "

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 packets stevia sugar Substitute (such as truvia)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine frozen mangoes, almond milk, sweetener, and vanilla extract in a high-capacity blender, such as a VitamixÂ®. Blend until mixture has the consistency of ice cream, 45 to 60 seconds.

Tips from the author

If you want to make a smoothie, increase the almond milk to 1 cup and blend until smooth.
